What is a free fall?

Literally, a free fall is what would happen when you release an object in the mid air and let it drop. I am too scared to cite any source, so feast your eyes with the correct definition at wikipedia.

Something you might not know about falling

The ISS (International Space Station) is constantly FALLING! But since it travels so fast, that it falls not towards the ground but around the earth. It resembles the process when you through a piece of stone away from a clif at an extremely fast speed, it falls down and match the curve of the earth, so it never lands.

Rain drops never kill people because they cannot accelerate too much! A falling object in the atmosphere cannot keep accelerating due to air resistance. It would stop accelerating at a certain speed known as the terminal velocity when air resistance is equilibrium to the gravitational force.
